What is “Enjoy Word Wolf”

Enjoy Word Wolf is a free App. You can play Word Wolf with your friends, (even remotely) - anywhere.

You do not need to install/register. It is totally free

Meet Enjoy Word Wolf.



What is special about Enjoy Word Wolf?

There are so many things to do to within the word wolf game. Previously all participants were required to install an app and the layout differences between different OS were overly complicated.

Enjoy Word Wolf is made to solve all your problems. No more stress. It is not even required that all participants install the App.



All you need to do is one of the players installs the App and selects “Play with friends”.

Just download the App to your home screen and you can play offline – your friends are not required to have the App. You can invite them from your App.

Play with friends



If you have friends wanting to play with you remote, you can select “Play online” to issue an URL and share it via LINE/Zoom. You can play with your friends regardless of the distance.

(※Please DO NOT close your browser while playing a game. )

(Timer might not work if you close)

Play online



You do not need to install the App

Enjoy Word Wolf is a browser-based App, you do not need to install the App.

All you need is a browser to connect to the App.

We use PWA technology to enable any users to download and add the App to your home screen via the browser.

▶How to install an App via browser

  • For Android devices

    • Open top page on Google Chrome

    Tap “Add Enjoy Word Wolf to Home screen” at the bottom edge of your screen. -pop up will appear.

    Then tap “Add”

    Enjoy Word Wolf would be installed and appeared to your home screen.

    Once you add it to your home screen, you can play “Play with friends” function even offline.

    Enjoy the game whenever you want!

  • For iOS

    • Open top page on Safari

    Select “Share” from a menu, then tap “Add to Home Screen”

    Enjoy Word Wolf would be installed and appeared to your home screen.

    Once you add it to your home screen, you can play “Play with friends” function even offline.

    Enjoy the game whenever you want!

You do not need to register or send personal information to us at all.

No need to worry about personal information leakage.
