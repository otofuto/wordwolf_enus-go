Enjoy Word Wolf FAQ

Below is our FAQs about Enjoy Word Wolf

Please feel free to contact us via the contact form.



How to play word wolf?

Click here to check rules of word wolf

How to play

How often do you update themes?

We update themes approximately every 3 days

We will let you know on TwitterTwitter once the theme is updated

Do I need to register to use the App?

None of the participants are required to register.

Generate and share a game URL to enter a game room and start a game.

Is the game child friendly? Are there are any inappropriate words?

It is a child friendly game. We do not use any curse or inappropriate words. There is nothing of a sexual, offensive or discriminatory nature.

Please let us know via the contact form if you have any queries.

Can we play this game offline?

You can play offline by adding the App into the home screen, select “Add to home screen” via Google Chrome.

How many people can play together?

Both “Play with your friends” and “Play online” does not have max players count.

(Large number of participates might cause server issue)

I do not understand the theme that I have been allocated.

We are sorry for any inconvenience.

Please let us know what the theme in question is via our contact form. We will then sort out the best way to resolve this.

Is there any charge in this App?

No, Enjoy Word Wolf is completely free.

Do I need to provide my personal information?

No, you do not need to provide either personal information nor Cookie information.

I have a problem with game play for some reason. What can I do?

We are sorry for any inconvenience.

Please let us know what the issue is via the contact form and we will sort out the best way to resolve.

Contact form

A theme I received was inappropriate to me, can you review this?

Please let us know what the theme is via the contact form, we will then sort out the best way to resolve.

Is anything additional required share the URL?

No, nothing additional is required.

You can share a URL via LINE, Facebook Messengers, Zoom and more. – whichever is your preferred messaging platform.

Where can I locate the latest update info?

Please check our Twitter account.

You can find us by searching “Enjoy Word Wolf”

Can I share recorded gameplay from this App on social media, YouTube etc?

No problem, we do not require any permission to play and post the game in any media channel.

However it would be much appreciated, (if you have a large audience) to liaise with us in regards to delivering the correct information to your audience. Feel free to use our in App contact form for this.
