About Ward Wolf Theme Category



There are 7 categories in Enjoy Word Wolf.

The theme will be well known things that everyone in the world would understand.

Kids friendly – We do not have any curse words or phrases at all.

Feel free to reach us out via our contact form if you have any queries.


The categories in the App are as follows;



Sports

The name of the sport or associated equipment related words are included.

If you like to watch or play any sports this is a theme for you.

Suitable for playing while watching sports.



Life

Tools you definitely will have used in everyday life; back in school etc…

Great for breaking the ice with your new friends. Playing this game for first time, everybody should easily know all the themes.



Romance

This theme has the words of love and romance

If you want to feel romance, this is the theme you should go ahead with.

Don’t worry, nothing sexual, weird or disturbing.



Food and Drink

This theme contains the names of food/drinks.

Suitable for kids as well.

We would recommend for you to spend a little longer in discussion time, as our range is massive!



Worlds

The name of the countries and cities are here.

If you are familiar with world geography, this is the theme for you!

Basic geography knowledge required.



Celebrity

Celebrities from t.v, movies, music, history.

Suitable for 15 years old above, some high difficulty themes are included.

Recommended longer discussion time.



Randoms

All themes are mixed, you will see what the category is when you receive it!

Recommended longer discussion time – you will never know what themes there will be to discuss as they always change.
